Who Are We?

Assistive Technologies is a club based at Poolesville High School whose goal is to provide our students with the opportunity to impact their community in a meaning ful way while also refining their technical skills in computer science and engineering

What Do We Do?

Assistive Technologies works with local companies who employ workers with disabilities in order to develop software or physical devices to aid in their work life

History behind AT Club?

The SMCS house at Poolesville has for many years competed in the Source America IDEATE competition in which students work with a worker with disabilities to develop a piece of assitive technology to help within their work life, however the competition has recently been discontinued.
Assistive Technologies aims to fill the space left from the conclusion of the IDEATE competition, and still give students an opportunity to have a positive impact their community.
